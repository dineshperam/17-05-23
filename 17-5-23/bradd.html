About Brad Pitt

Brad Pitt is an American actor and film producer. He has received numerous awards and nominations including an Academy Award as producer under his own company, Plan B Entertainment.

Pitt has been consistently cited as one of the most influential and powerful people in the American entertainment industry. He has appeared in a wide range of films, including Fight Club, Troy, Inglourious Basterds, Moneyball, and Once Upon a Time in Hollywood.

Brad Pitt's talent and charm have made him one of the most recognizable and beloved actors of his generation.
